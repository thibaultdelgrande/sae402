Working-class city

One of my dear friend send me a letter where he give me a little gift with this instructions. To open my gift, you need to draw numbers with this following paths : Start at 43 rue de l’Arbre, go to the left, then down, right, down, left and then stop at the next crossroad. Start at 2 Passage de l’Orme, go to the right, then down, left, down, right and then stop at the next crossroad. Start at 10 Passage des Allouette, go to the right, then down, left, down, right, up, left, up and then stop at the next crossroad. Okay now, use this numbers in this order and use the code to unlock it. Good luck!
